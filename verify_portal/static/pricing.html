Frequently asked questions

Can I use the open source version in production?

Yes. EPI Recorder is MIT licensed. You can use it in production, modify it, and distribute it. The open source CLI does full Annex IV compliance. Pro and above add hosted services and support.

Do I need the Enterprise tier for EU AI Act compliance?

No. All tiers include Annex IV compliance artifacts with Ed25519 signatures, hash chains, and SCITT transparency receipts. Enterprise adds institutional features for regulated organizations.

What payment methods do you accept?

Lemon Squeezy handles all payments — credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay depending on your region.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime by contacting us. You keep access until the end of your billing period. 14-day refund window for new subscriptions.
