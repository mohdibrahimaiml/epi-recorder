FAQ

Common questions

Is the open source version really free?

Yes. The core epi-recorder, verifier, and fault analyzer are MIT licensed. Use them commercially, modify them, redistribute them — no restrictions.

What happens to my .epi files if I stop paying?

Your .epi files are yours forever. They verify in any browser without our infrastructure, without our servers, and without an active subscription. That's the point.

Do you store my AI execution data?

No. EPI runs entirely on your machine. The .epi file is created locally, stored locally, and verified locally. We never see your prompts, your outputs, or your data.

When will Team and Enterprise tiers launch?

Team tier is targeted for Q3 2026. Enterprise follows in Q4. Join the waitlist to get early access and founding partner pricing.
