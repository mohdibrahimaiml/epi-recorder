The problem

Your AI is making decisions.
You can't prove what it decided.

⚖️

Regulatory exposure

The EU AI Act, CFPB guidance, and FCA rules require documented evidence of AI decisions affecting people. Logs in a vendor dashboard are hard to package and verify independently.

🔍

Fragmented audit trails

LLM call in one tool. Business logic in another. Human review in a third. When regulators ask, engineers spend weeks reconstructing what happened.

🚨

No tamper evidence

Even if you find the logs, you still need to show they were preserved consistently. Screenshots and ad hoc exports are difficult to verify. You need cryptographic proof of integrity.

August 2, 2026 — key EU AI Act obligations for high-risk AI systems are approaching. Teams using AI in loan decisions, HR screening, or insurance underwriting should prepare durable decision records. The deadline is approaching.

How it works

Three lines of code.
A defensible record forever.

01

Capture

Point your existing AI SDK at the EPI gateway. LLM calls, tool invocations, and decision events can be captured automatically — minimal application changes.

OPENAI_BASE_URL=http://localhost:8787/v1
python my_agent.py
02

Record

EPI packages the decision trail into a portable .epi artifact — a signed case file containing captured steps, model outputs, policy context, and human review where available.

epi gateway export --case-id loan-4821
→ loan-4821.epi (signed, 6 files verified)
03

Verify

Reviewers can verify that the artifact has not been tampered with. No EPI account required.

epi verify loan-4821.epi
→ VERIFIED ✓ Trust Level: HIGH
→ Ed25519 · 6 files · SHA-256

What EPI captures

Everything in one artifact. Signed at the moment of decision.

🧾

Full decision trail

Every LLM prompt and response, tool call and result, agent step, and business logic outcome — in order, timestamped.

🔐

Ed25519 signature

The artifact is cryptographically signed at creation. Any modification — even a single byte — invalidates the signature.

👤

Human review record

Who reviewed the decision, what they decided, when, and why — embedded in the same signed artifact.

📋

Policy applied

Which epi_policy.json was in effect at the time of the decision, and whether the AI's behaviour complied.

🌐

Works with any LLM

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, or any OpenAI-compatible API. Minimal application changes: point your SDK at the EPI gateway.

📦

Portable forever

A .epi file is a self-contained SQLite/CBOR Envelope. No EPI account needed to verify it — today, or in 10 years.
