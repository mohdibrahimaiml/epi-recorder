Frequently asked questions

Why is the .epi format and browser verifier free?

The .epi format is an open standard — like PDF or HTTP. Free distribution means every .epi file advertises EPI. Every browser becomes a verifier. The format spreads through use, not through pricing. Paid plans add hosted infrastructure on top of the already-adopted free product.

What if I outgrow the Pro plan?

Upgrade to Compliance at any time. Your evidence history and API keys remain intact. If you need something custom, the Enterprise tier or a services engagement can cover it.

Do I need a paid plan to verify .epi files?

No. Every .epi file embeds its own browser verifier. Drop it into any browser and it verifies itself — zero servers, zero accounts, zero cost. Paid plans add hosted storage, team collaboration, and compliance tooling for teams that need those at scale.

How does SCITT anchoring work?

When you verify through the hosted portal, the evidence hash is registered in a SCITT transparency ledger with a Merkle inclusion proof. This provides cryptographic proof that the evidence existed at a specific point in time — critical for regulatory audits with multi-year retention requirements.

What compliance frameworks do you support?

AIUC-1 (6 trust domains), EU AI Act Article 12, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, and SOC 2. Custom framework mapping is available on the Enterprise plan. Every verification report includes AIUC-1 domain mapping by default.
