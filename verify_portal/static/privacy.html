Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

Nothing by default. EPI Labs does not collect personal data when you visit epilabs.org. We don't use tracking cookies, fingerprinting scripts, or third-party advertising trackers.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, cookie-free analytics service. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track users across sessions or websites. We see aggregated page view counts and referrer sources only.

Email signups

When you sign up for the pilot program, we collect your email address and use case. This information is stored only for pilot communication purposes. We never share, sell, or transfer your email to third parties. You can request deletion at any time by emailing mohdibrahim@epilabs.org.

The EPI Recorder CLI

The epi-recorder command-line tool and Python SDK run entirely on your machine. They do not send data to EPI Labs servers. The browser-based verification at epilabs.org/verify runs entirely client-side — your .epi files are never uploaded.

Hosted services

Enterprise customers may opt into our hosted SCITT transparency service. Data transmitted to this service is limited to cryptographic hashes and signatures required for SCITT anchoring. No LLM prompts, responses, or agent decisions are transmitted.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests: mohdibrahim@epilabs.org
