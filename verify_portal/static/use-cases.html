Industries

Where AI decisions need proof

EPI is built for any domain where an AI decision carries weight. When the question comes, the answer is a sealed file.

💰

Financial Services

AI Lending · Fraud · Credit Scoring

When an AI model approves a mortgage or flags a transaction, regulators need the full execution — not a summary. EPI captures model endpoint, prompt, response, tool invocations, and business rules. The sealed file can be verified years after the model is deprecated and the infrastructure retired.

SR 11-7FINRAFCRAECB
🏥

Healthcare & Medical AI

FDA · Clinical · Diagnostics

FDA reviews AI/ML-enabled medical devices through PCCPs. Manufacturers must prove a model ran exactly as described in their 510(k). EPI produces a signed snapshot of every execution — the model fingerprint, inputs, outputs, and environment — that any notified body can verify offline.

FDA 21 CFR 11PCCPMDR
📋

Insurance Underwriting

Pricing · Claims · Governance

AI-driven claims processing faces growing scrutiny. When a claim is denied or a premium set by AI, insurers must produce the full decision trail. EPI seals every input, model response, rule evaluation, and override — verified independently without internal system access.

NAIC AINY DFSEIOPA
🛡

AI Safety & Alignment

Red-Teaming · Eval · Audit

AI safety teams need reproducible evidence that a model was evaluated under specific conditions. EPI captures the complete eval run — model version, prompts, responses, safety classifier outputs — in a signed artifact that auditors can verify without access to the model.

AIUC-1SOC 2ISO 42001
🏛

Government & Public Sector

Benefits · Procurement · Law

Government agencies using AI for benefits or procurement must demonstrate due process. EPI creates a forensic record of every automated decision — including human review — that oversight bodies can examine independently.

OMB M-24-10NIST AI RMF
⚖

Legal Tech & eDiscovery

Contract AI · Litigation

Firms using AI for document review or contract analysis need evidence that withstands adversarial scrutiny. EPI seals the complete decision process — prompt, response, reasoning, citations — into a file that opposing counsel and judges can independently verify.

FRCP 26(g)ABA 483
