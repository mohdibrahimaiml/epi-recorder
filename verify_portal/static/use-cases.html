Industry Deep Dive

Why portable evidence matters once AI decisions have real business consequences.

HC Healthcare & Life Sciences

Problem: FDA PCCP & Liability

Use of AI in medical devices (SaMD) now requires a Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP). Manufacturers must prove that every model update matches their pre-validated protocol. Current manual logging is error-prone and rejected by auditors.

EPI Solution: Automated Validation

EPI creates a cryptographically signed snapshot of the code, data, and environment for every single run. It provides the immutable evidence that the FDA demands, proving that the executed code matches the approved protocol exactly.

FIN Financial Services

Problem: SR 11-7 & Audit

Banking regulations (SR 11-7) mandate rigorous Model Risk Management. Banks must explain why an algorithm denied a loan. "Black box" AI models creates massive liability and regulatory fines for lack of explainability.

EPI Solution: Non-Repudiation

EPI acts as an independent black-box recorder. Its Ed25519 signatures enable Non-Repudiation - a mathematical proof that the record hasn't been altered. This is the "gold standard" audit trail for compliance teams.

LAW Legal & Chain of Custody

Problem: Digital Admissibility

Screenshots and logs are easily faked. In court, "deepfakes" and AI-generated content are challenging traditional Chain of Custody. Without cryptographic proof, digital evidence is increasingly inadmissible.

EPI Solution: Cryptographic Seal

Every .epi file is sealed with a private key. This creates an unbroken Chain of Custody from the moment of creation. It proves who ran the model, when, and that the evidence is 100% authentic.
