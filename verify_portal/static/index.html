What is EPI?

EPI (Episodic Process Integrity) is an open protocol for cryptographically recording, verifying, and attesting to the behavior of AI systems. Every decision, every guardrail check, every redaction — sealed and independently verifiable.

Trust Anchors

EPI Labs operates a did:web identity that anchors all official attestations. The trust chain resolves to this domain.

/.well-known/did.json — DID document
