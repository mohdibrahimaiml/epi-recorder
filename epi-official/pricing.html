Frequently asked questions

Can I use the open source version in production?

Yes. EPI Recorder is MIT licensed. The Starter tier includes the full CLI and SDK. Pro and Advanced add hosted services and support.

Do I need the Advanced tier for EU AI Act compliance?

No. All tiers include Annex IV compliance artifacts with Ed25519 signatures, hash chains, and SCITT transparency receipts. Advanced adds institutional features for regulated organizations.

What payment methods do you accept?

Paddle handles all payments — credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and local payment methods depending on your country.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings or by contacting us. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
